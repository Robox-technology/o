Who we are

Built by builders, for builders.

RoboX Technology is an independent robotics team focused on designing and engineering real-world robotic systems from scratch. We started as a small group of students with a shared obsession: building machines that actually work.

From Arduino and ESP8266 prototypes to fully autonomous robots, every system we create is designed, wired, coded, and tested entirely in-house. We believe in hands-on engineering over off-the-shelf solutions.

Today, we build robots for competitions, client projects, and our own ongoing innovation research — and we make them available for sale to schools, labs, and enthusiasts.
