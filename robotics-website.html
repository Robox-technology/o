Who we are

Built by builders,
for the real world.

RoboX Technology is an independent robotics engineering team that designs and builds intelligent robotic systems entirely from scratch. We started with a simple belief: real engineering means understanding every wire, every line of code, and every mechanical joint in what you build.

Over the past 6+ years, we've grown from a small group of passionate builders into a team that has completed 22+ robotics projects — from autonomous navigation bots to IoT-connected smart systems and precision agriculture robots.

We compete, we innovate, and we build for clients. Every robot we sell is one we've already tested, refined, and trusted with our own name on it.
